HM YM YM Restaurant

Founded in 1998, Guangzhou Sanhe Decoration Design Co., Ltd. adheres to the design concept of Limited Space & Unlimited Creativity, is committed to the professional design and commercial planning of hotel, restaurants, noble villas, office buildings, entertainment and leisure clubs and other commercial spaces. We have successively won many awards in China's national, provincial and municipal design competitions. Through the efforts of all employees, we have obtained the "Consumer Relax Enterprise in 2003, Consumer Satisfaction Unit of Guangdong Province in 2004, Honesty Enterprise of Decoration Industry in 2005, Famous Decoration Company in Guangzhou, Guangzhou Environmental Protection & Declaration Enterprise and other special honors; became Member Unit of China Architectural Decoration Association, Member Unit of Guangdong Decoration Industry Association, South China Executive Director Unit of IFDA International Decoration Association, Best Honesty Enterprise in Guangdong, Executive Director Unit of Guangdong Credit Association, and won the title of Trustworthy Enterprise in Guangdong for four consecutive years. Architectural decoration engineering design B and construction grade II qualifications issued by Guangdong Provincial Department of Housing and Urban-Rural Development. Read more
