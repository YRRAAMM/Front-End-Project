Recipe

pan cake

  • 4 Tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 cub of milk fat free
  • 1 cub of white flour
  • 1 Teaspoon baking powder

pan cake

  • 1 Tablespoon of large oil
  • 1 Egg
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch vanilla

Steps

1- Whisk the egg, salt, vanilla and sugar with a fork or regular egg beater, then add the milk and whisk.

2- Sift the flour and baking powder together, then add to the mixture and stir well, add an oil spoon, and then mix it well with the manual paddle or fork.

3- In a non-stick frying pan or Tefal wipe with a little oil and then begin to put a mixture in it the amount of a cup of coffee. menna

4-Leave on a low heat to brown the bottom of the pancake, then stir on the other side to simmer, repeat the process until the amount of the mixture is finished.
